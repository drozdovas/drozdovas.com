Hello World!

2022-06-12

Hi, my name is Dima and welcome to my first ever official blog post.

Starting a blog is not as glorious as Vlogging nowadays, but it’s still a good form of expression.

My goal is to improve my writing and storytelling by creating a blog post on a regular basis. I’ll try and aim for 3 a week.

Repetition and consistency often leads to success. If you are trying to learn or start something new, here’s a tip:

  1. Schedule a time, let’s say Mon and Wed 7pm.
  2. Even if you don’t feel like it, force yourself to start dabbling.
  3. Once you catch momentum, a flow state is not too far off.
  4. Give it some time, a week or two. If you’re not enjoying what you are doing, don’t be afraid to drop it.

There are so many things out there to try. Though I’m secretly hoping writing is something that will stick with me.
