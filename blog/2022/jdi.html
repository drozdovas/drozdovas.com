Just Do It!

2022-07-03

Here I am, on a holiday, on a sunny Sunday afternoon. Thinking to myself that I set a goal to write 3 blog posts a week and that I absolutely need to do this.

There’s no one to hold me accountable, so potentially I could skip it and no one would know. Up until today, nobody read my blogs.

But there’s something deep down in me that keeps screaming. “Do it!” You have a minute or ten, which means we will spend that time thinking about things you haven’t done.

So instead of fighting my inner “productivity lord” - it’s easier to sit down and complete whatever I set out to do.

I’ve noticed recently that the best time to do something is now. If it’s not done, it stays on my mind and bothers me.

Special case scenario. I have a very young son and it’s close to impossible to say - i’ll do it later. Fact is, you don’t know what later will look like.

So the lesson here is, do it as soon as possible. And don’t waste the mental energy holding onto thoughts.
