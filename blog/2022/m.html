Minimalism

2022-07-06

I have this philosophy of life that minimalism reduces the daily strain on our minds.

I own a tiny iPhone, which I primary use for scrolling Twitter. I have a single 24” monitor to work on, one app at a time. This logic applies pretty much to everything I own and use. 

The less things you have, the less you need to worry. Which leads to more focus. Less is therefore more?

The is one of the reasons why these blog posts are so small and to the point.
