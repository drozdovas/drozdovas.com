Should you have a child?

2022-06-30

This question appeared only briefly in my mind. Then I decided that the pain of regret would be horrible once I’m at the age when it’s no longer possible.

So low and behold in Dec2021 my handsome son was born.

Since then I had a few thought about the matter and here’s the answer to the main question.

If you wake every morning feeling great, everything is going your way - go for it. Because as soon as you fall into this rabbit hole, things will become difficult and there’s no room for other life events/issues. You will have to bid your Comfortable Life farewell. 

Sometimes it feels like the pain will stop soon, but it never does. A new day brings a new challenge. How long this will last I cannot say, yet…

But somewhere deep inside I know, it will be all worth it in the end.
