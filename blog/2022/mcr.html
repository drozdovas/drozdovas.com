Market Crash - Recession

2022-06-14

Ladies and gentlemen, it’s here. Stock and Crypto market is crashing.

How long this will last - nobody knows. But looking back in time, after each crash the market went up above the previous peak.

So if you were waiting for the dip, it’s finally here. Here's a quote from Warren Buffett:
 “Be fearful when others are greedy, and be greedy only when others are fearful.”

I will take a wild guess, by next March we might see a reversal? In the meantime, stay strong! And never bet what you cannot afford to lose.
